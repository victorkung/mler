GET INVOLVED

Volunteering as a Bar Exam Tutor is one of the more familiar ways that MLER can use your help. However, there are several other ways you can stay involved:

  • Come hang out! Join us for our quarterly mixers and other events.

  • Coordinate a site visit to your law school, law firm, or organization.

  • Enroll MLER in your employer’s gift matching program or charity initiative.

  • Take our program, present your experience with MLER at our upcoming school visits.

  • Make an in-kind donation (e.g., printing costs) to help facilitate the Bar Exam Preparation Program. Please visit the “Donations” page for more details.

  • Follow us on Facebook or Twitter and provide us with insightful feedback. We would love to hear your ideas!
